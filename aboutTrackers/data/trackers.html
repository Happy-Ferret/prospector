about:trackers remembers the sites you visit that are being tracked and blocks trackers that know too much about you.

  • Trackers following you across sites will be completely blocked.
  • Sites you visit that are also trackers & trackers that following you across sites will not get their tracking cookies.
  • Sites without cookies are not blocked because they are less likely to be trackers.
